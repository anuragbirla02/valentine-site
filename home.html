I don’t promise perfection,
but I promise honesty, effort,
and choosing you — every single day.

In all the chaos of the world,
you feel like my calm.

Will you be mine? ❤️

Somewhere between our conversations,
you became a place my heart feels safe.
Loving you doesn’t feel loud,
it feels calm, steady, and right.
You didn’t change my world overnight,
you slowly became the best part of it.
If love had a feeling,
it would feel like talking to you.

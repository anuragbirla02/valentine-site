Hi Love ❤️

I could've sent a message. I could've sent flowers. But I wanted to create something that stays — just like what I feel for you.

You walked into my life quietly,
but somehow changed everything.

You make ordinary days feel lighter,
and difficult ones feel possible.

I may not say it every day,
but choosing you is my favourite habit.

Happy Valentine's Day ❤️

No matter where life takes us, I want you to know — you were chosen. Every single day.
